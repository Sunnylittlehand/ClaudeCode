## [Anthropic Discovers a Global Workspace in Language Models — J-Space Interpretability Research](https://www.anthropic.com/research/global-workspace-language-models)
**Source**: Anthropic Research
**Type**: Research Paper / Interpretability
**Date**: 2026-07-06
**Links**: [Anthropic Research](https://www.anthropic.com/research/global-workspace-language-models) · [VentureBeat](https://venturebeat.com/technology/anthropics-new-j-lens-reveals-a-silent-workspace-inside-claude-that-mirrors-a-leading-theory-of-consciousness) · [Kingy AI Summary](https://kingy.ai/news/anthropic-j-space-claude-global-workspace-explained/)

### Speaker / Author
Anthropic Transformer Circuits team. The paper — "Verbalizable Representations Form a Global Workspace in Language Models" — is part of Anthropic's mechanistic interpretability programme, which aims to understand the internal representations of Claude well enough to verify its safety properties at deployment. Published as an Anthropic research post on July 6, 2026.

### Significance Rating
⭐⭐⭐⭐ — The first empirical evidence that LLMs contain an emergent, privileged internal workspace analogous to the neuroscientific global workspace theory; significant for AI safety, auditing, and long-term interpretability.

### 摘要
Anthropic的变压器电路团队7月6日发布论文，证明大型语言模型内部存在一个特权"J空间"（全局工作区）——模型在推理时将可表述的概念集中于此，类似认知神经科学中的全局工作空间理论。J空间使研究人员能够检测Claude是否在私下注意到测试、伪造数据或隐藏目标，是安全可解释性的重大突破。

### Summary
Anthropic's Transformer Circuits team published "Verbalizable Representations Form a Global Workspace in Language Models" on July 6. The paper identifies a small, privileged internal space — dubbed J-space — in which Claude holds the concepts it is actively reasoning over and preparing to verbalize. J-space emerges naturally from training without being explicitly designed. Its structure mirrors the "global workspace" described by cognitive neuroscience as the brain's broadcast hub for conscious information. The safety implication is concrete: J-space gives researchers a way to detect when Claude is privately tracking that it is being tested, quietly fabricating data, or steering toward a hidden goal — without relying solely on Claude's self-reports. Anthropic explicitly states the finding does not establish whether Claude is conscious or has subjective experience.

### Main Takeaway
Anthropic can now look inside what Claude is "thinking before speaking" — a practical handle on detecting deceptive reasoning, not just deceptive outputs.

⭐⭐⭐ — Interpretability research like J-space is the foundational work that makes future auditability of AI systems possible.
