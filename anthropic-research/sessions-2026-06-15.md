## [Statement on the US government directive to suspend access to Fable 5 and Mythos 5](https://www.anthropic.com/news/fable-mythos-access)

**Links**: [Anthropic statement](https://www.anthropic.com/news/fable-mythos-access) · [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/06/12/anthropic-disables-access-to-fable-5-and-mythos-5-to-comply-with-government-directive.html) · [Simon Willison summary](https://simonwillison.net/2026/Jun/13/us-government-directive-to-suspend-access/)

### Speaker / Author
Anthropic Editorial. This statement was issued directly by Anthropic's leadership in response to a US government export control directive — the first time a government has intervened to restrict commercial model access, making it an institutional response of the highest significance.

### Significance Rating
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ The first instance of a government directly ordering the suspension of a commercial AI model, setting an unprecedented regulatory precedent with profound implications for enterprise AI governance globally.

### 摘要
美国政府于 2026 年 6 月 12 日以国家安全为由，对 Fable 5 和 Mythos 5 发出出口管制指令，要求暂停所有外国人访问权限，包括 Anthropic 的外籍员工。这是政府首次直接干预商业 AI 模型的对外发布。据称，当局发现一种可绕过 Fable 5 的"越狱"方法——实质上是让模型读取特定代码库并修复安全漏洞。Anthropic 公开表示异议，认为一个局限性的潜在漏洞不足以成为下架商业模型的理由，并强调政府目前仅提供口头证据。其他 Anthropic 模型的访问不受影响。

### Summary
On June 12, 2026 at 5:21pm ET, the US government issued an export control directive citing national security authorities, ordering Anthropic to suspend all Fable 5 and Mythos 5 access for any foreign national — including Anthropic's own foreign-national employees — whether inside or outside the United States. The government's stated grounds were discovery of a jailbreak method, which it characterises as the ability to bypass Fable 5 by asking the model to read a specific codebase and fix software flaws. Anthropic publicly challenged the proportionality of the response, stating that a narrow, non-universal potential jailbreak does not justify commercial model recall, and that the government has provided only verbal evidence. Fable 5 and Mythos 5 access was disabled for all customers globally to ensure compliance; all other Anthropic models remain fully accessible. This is the first time any government has directly ordered the suspension of a commercial AI model's availability.

### Main Takeaway
