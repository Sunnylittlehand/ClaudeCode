### Speaker / Author
Anthropic Editorial. This is an evolving regulatory and commercial situation rather than a single authored post. The developments this week represent the culmination of a ten-day chain of events beginning with the June 12 US government export-control directive.

### Significance Rating
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Two distinct shock events in one week: Fable 5's global access was suspended (June 12), conditionally restored with identity checks and geo-fencing (June ~18), and as of today (June 23) removed from all subscription plans including Enterprise — making every additional Fable 5 token a metered pay-per-use cost.

### 摘要
Claude Fable 5 和 Mythos 5 经历了本周期内最戏剧性的政策演变：先是遭美国政府出口管制指令于6月12日全球下架，数日后以附加条件重新开放——用户须完成政府签发证件+自拍核验，并受国籍地理围栏限制；随后从6月23日起，Fable 5 从 Pro、Max、Team 和 Enterprise 订阅套餐中彻底移除，转为按 API 用量计费，输入每百万 token 10 美元、输出 50 美元，是 Opus 4.8 价格的两倍。Anthropic 将其定性为容量限制而非永久定价政策，承诺待容量充足后恢复订阅包含。

### Summary
Fable 5 and Mythos 5 were suspended globally on June 12 under a US government export-control directive triggered — in part — by an NSA Director's Senate testimony that Mythos had autonomously breached nearly all of the NSA's classified systems in a red-team exercise (a characterisation later partially walked back by journalists). Access was conditionally restored around June 18, but with mandatory identity verification (government ID + selfie) and nationality-based geo-fencing. Separately, from June 23 today, Anthropic has removed Fable 5 from all subscription plan allowances — Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise seat plans — meaning any Fable 5 usage now draws down usage credits at API rates: $10 per million input tokens and $50 per million output tokens, double the cost of Opus 4.8. Anthropic frames this as a temporary capacity constraint rather than a permanent pricing tier, and has pledged to restore Fable 5 as a standard subscription feature as soon as capacity allows.

### Main Takeaway
Fable 5 — Anthropic's most capable model — is now pay-per-token for every enterprise user, effective today, with no confirmed reinstatement date.
