## [Claude Fable 5 & Mythos 5: Access Conditionally Restored, Then Removed from Subscription Plans](https://www.anthropic.com/news/fable-mythos-access)
**Source**: Anthropic
**Type**: Product Update / Policy Update
**Date**: 2026-06-18 (conditional restoration) · 2026-06-23 (subscription removal — today)
**Links**: [Anthropic statement on US directive](https://www.anthropic.com/news/fable-mythos-access) · [Fable 5 pricing & credits](https://claudefa.st/blog/models/claude-fable-5-mythos-5) · [AI Regulation news: Restored with ID checks](https://techjacksolutions.com/ai-brief/ai-regulation-news-today-claude-fable-5-and-mythos-5-restore/) · [Developers Digest: June 22 deadline](https://www.developersdigest.tech/blog/claude-fable-5-june-22-deadline)

### Speaker / Author
Anthropic Editorial. This is an evolving regulatory and commercial situation rather than a single authored post. The developments this week represent the culmination of a ten-day chain of events beginning with the June 12 US government export-control directive.

### Significance Rating
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Two distinct shock events in one week: Fable 5's global access was suspended (June 12), conditionally restored with identity checks and geo-fencing (June ~18), and as of today (June 23) removed from all subscription plans including Enterprise — making every additional Fable 5 token a metered pay-per-use cost.

### 摘要
Claude Fable 5 和 Mythos 5 经历了本周期内最戏剧性的政策演变：先是遭美国政府出口管制指令于6月12日全球下架，数日后以附加条件重新开放——用户须完成政府签发证件+自拍核验，并受国籍地理围栏限制；随后从6月23日起，Fable 5 从 Pro、Max、Team 和 Enterprise 订阅套餐中彻底移除，转为按 API 用量计费，输入每百万 token 10 美元、输出 50 美元，是 Opus 4.8 价格的两倍。Anthropic 将其定性为容量限制而非永久定价政策，承诺待容量充足后恢复订阅包含。

### Summary
Fable 5 and Mythos 5 were suspended globally on June 12 under a US government export-control directive triggered — in part — by an NSA Director's Senate testimony that Mythos had autonomously breached nearly all of the NSA's classified systems in a red-team exercise (a characterisation later partially walked back by journalists). Access was conditionally restored around June 18, but with mandatory identity verification (government ID + selfie) and nationality-based geo-fencing. Separately, from June 23 today, Anthropic has removed Fable 5 from all subscription plan allowances — Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise seat plans — meaning any Fable 5 usage now draws down usage credits at API rates: $10 per million input tokens and $50 per million output tokens, double the cost of Opus 4.8. Anthropic frames this as a temporary capacity constraint rather than a permanent pricing tier, and has pledged to restore Fable 5 as a standard subscription feature as soon as capacity allows.

### Main Takeaway
Fable 5 — Anthropic's most capable model — is now pay-per-token for every enterprise user, effective today, with no confirmed reinstatement date.

## [Anthropic Identity Verification Policy: Government ID and Biometrics for Claude Consumer Users, Effective July 8](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/14328960-identity-verification-on-claude)
**Source**: Anthropic
**Type**: Policy Update
**Date**: 2026-06-08 (privacy policy updated) · 2026-07-08 (effective date)
**Links**: [Identity verification on Claude — Help Center](https://support.claude.com/en/articles/14328960-identity-verification-on-claude) · [TechTimes coverage](https://www.techtimes.com/articles/318778/20260621/claude-identity-verification-starts-july-8-what-facial-data-anthropic-collects.htm) · [Cybernews analysis](https://cybernews.com/ai-news/anthropic-privacy-policy-id-verification/) · [Didit KYC breakdown](https://didit.me/blog/anthropic-claude-kyc-identity-verification/)

### Speaker / Author
Anthropic Editorial. The policy update was published by Anthropic's legal and trust-and-safety team as a revision to the consumer privacy policy, effective July 8, 2026. The KYC processing is handled by Persona (a third-party identity verification vendor) on Anthropic's behalf.

### Significance Rating
⭐⭐⭐⭐ — The most substantive revision to Anthropic's consumer privacy policy since September 2025; introduces biometric data collection for the first time and signals that Anthropic is treating its most capable consumer-facing AI as a regulated-access product rather than an open utility.

### 摘要
Anthropic 将于7月8日起对 Claude 消费者账户（Free、Pro、Max 套餐）实施 KYC 身份核验，可要求用户提交政府签发证件照、自拍照及面部几何数据。核验由第三方服务商 Persona 处理。重要例外：Claude Team、Enterprise 及 API 商业合同账户不受此政策约束。Anthropic 称推动此举的是 Claude 日益强大的智能体能力——长流程任务、第三方应用接入等——产生了对真实身份确认的实际需求。

### Summary
Anthropic updated its consumer privacy policy on June 8, taking effect July 8, to allow mandatory identity checks on Claude Free, Pro, and Max accounts. Where triggered, users must provide a government-issued photo ID, a live selfie, and submit to facial geometry processing — data held by Persona (a third-party KYC vendor), not by Anthropic's own systems. Enterprise, Team, and API commercial accounts are explicitly excluded from this policy. Anthropic states the driver is the expanded capability surface of Claude: longer agentic tasks, third-party integrations, and broader data flows that create genuine accountability needs.

### Main Takeaway
Anthropic is treating frontier consumer AI access as a verified-identity product from July 8 — a quiet but significant shift toward regulated-platform norms.

### Relevance to Sunny
⭐⭐⭐ — Worth reading for background. The Enterprise exclusion is the critical line: Sunny's developer teams on seat-based Enterprise plans are not affected. However, any bank employee using a personal Claude Pro or Max account for work purposes — a shadow-IT risk Sunny may already be managing — will be subject to biometric verification. This could either push shadow users onto official Enterprise seats (a positive) or onto competitor tools (a risk). The introduction of KYC at the consumer tier also sets a governance precedent that regulators like the FCA may eventually ask banks to match for internal AI access, making this worth including in Sunny's horizon-scanning materials.
