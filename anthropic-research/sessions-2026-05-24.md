### 摘要
Anthropic 首届开发者大会在旧金山召开，发布三项重磅功能：多智能体编排（Multi-agent Orchestration）、任务成果评估（Outcomes）以及"梦境自学"系统（Dreaming）。其中，Dreaming 允许智能体在两次任务之间自动回顾历史会话、提炼规律并写入记忆，使下一次执行更聪明——法律 AI 公司 Harvey 试点后任务完成率提升约 6 倍。这标志着 AI 辅助编程从"单次对话"范式向"持续进化的自主工程师"转变。

### Summary
Anthropic's first developer conference launched three capabilities that together move Claude from a reactive coding assistant to an autonomous engineering platform. **Multi-agent Orchestration** lets a lead agent decompose complex tasks and delegate to specialist sub-agents running in parallel, each with its own isolated context. **Outcomes** lets you write a success rubric and a separate Claude instance graders the agent's output in a loop until the rubric is satisfied. **Dreaming** is a scheduled background process that reviews an agent's completed sessions, extracts patterns, and writes new memory entries so the next session starts smarter — legal AI startup Harvey ran the pilot and saw task completion rates rise ~6×. Rate limits for Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans were simultaneously doubled, and a compute partnership with SpaceX Colossus 1 was announced to support the capacity demand.

### Main Takeaway
Anthropic has transformed Claude from a coding assistant into a self-improving agent platform where fleets of AIs can decompose, delegate, evaluate, and learn — across sessions, autonomously.
