### 摘要
Anthropic 首届开发者大会在旧金山召开，发布三项重磅功能：多智能体编排（Multi-agent Orchestration）、任务成果评估（Outcomes）以及"梦境自学"系统（Dreaming）。其中，Dreaming 允许智能体在两次任务之间自动回顾历史会话、提炼规律并写入记忆，使下一次执行更聪明——法律 AI 公司 Harvey 试点后任务完成率提升约 6 倍。这标志着 AI 辅助编程从"单次对话"范式向"持续进化的自主工程师"转变。

### Summary

Rate limits for Pro, Max, Team, and Enterprise plans were simultaneously doubled, and a compute partnership with SpaceX Colossus 1 was announced to support the capacity demand.

### Main Takeaway

## [Andrej Karpathy: From Vibe Coding to Agentic Engineering](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=96jN2OCOfLs)
**Source**: Andrej Karpathy
**Type**: YouTube / Conference Talk (Sequoia AI Ascent 2026)
**Date**: 2026-04-30 (published; event held late April 2026)
**Links**: [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=96jN2OCOfLs) · [Blog summary](https://karpathy.bearblog.dev/sequoia-ascent-2026/) · [Analysis: Frank's World](https://www.franksworld.com/2026/05/01/andrej-karpathy-on-the-evolution-from-vibe-coding-to-agentic-engineering/)

### Speaker / Author
Andrej Karpathy, in conversation with Sequoia partner Stephanie Zhan at AI Ascent 2026. At the time of this talk Karpathy was founder of Eureka Labs (now at Anthropic from May 19). He coined the term "vibe coding" earlier in 2026 and has since become the go-to voice for explaining the practical implications of LLM-powered development to both technical and non-technical audiences.

### Significance Rating
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Landmark educational content that defines the new professional discipline of AI-era software engineering; essential conceptual vocabulary for anyone overseeing developer teams using AI coding tools.

### 摘要
Karpathy 在 Sequoia AI Ascent 2026 上划出了一条关键分界线："氛围编程"（Vibe Coding）降低了门槛，让任何人都能用自然语言创造软件；而"智能体工程"（Agentic Engineering）则提升了上限，是专业人员在协调多个不可靠 AI 智能体时，如何确保正确性、安全性、品味与可维护性的新兴职业学科。他以 MenuGen 的支付漏洞为例——智能体用邮件匹配 Stripe 购买记录与 Google 账户，代码可运行但系统设计糟糕——指出人类工程师的核心价值在于坚守正确的系统设计，而不仅是让代码跑通。他对自己作为"速度最快的程序员"的身份感到落后，预示着行业角色的深刻转型。

### Summary
Karpathy draws a precise and important distinction between two modes of AI-assisted development. **Vibe coding** (a term he introduced earlier this year) democratises software creation — almost anyone can describe what they want and AI produces it, raising the floor for participation. **Agentic engineering** is the emerging professional discipline required to harness fleets of fallible AI agents while preserving correctness, security, taste, and maintainability — raising the ceiling for what skilled engineers can achieve. His MenuGen case study is instructive: an agent matched Stripe purchases to Google accounts via email address — plausible code, terrible system design — and only a human insisting on persistent user IDs caught it. Karpathy's central argument is that the human's irreplaceable role shifts from *writing* code to *holding the design and correctness bar* as agents generate increasingly large volumes of plausible-but-wrong outputs.

### Main Takeaway
Vibe coding is the on-ramp; agentic engineering is the profession — and Karpathy's framework tells you exactly what humans still need to do when AI writes most of the code.
