### Summary
Anthropic closed a $65 billion Series H round led by Altimeter Capital, Dragoneer, Greenoaks, and Sequoia Capital, lifting its post-money valuation to $965 billion — leapfrogging OpenAI as the most valuable private AI company. The round includes $15 billion from hyperscalers including a $5 billion commitment from Amazon, and $15 billion more from strategic hardware partners including Micron, Samsung, and SK Hynix. May 2026 revenue run-rate hit approximately $47 billion, up roughly four-fold year-on-year from ~$10 billion. Proceeds will advance safety and interpretability research, expand compute, and scale enterprise products — all ahead of what is now a near-certain IPO.

### Main Takeaway
