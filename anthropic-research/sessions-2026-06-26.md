### 摘要
OpenAI 与博通联合发布"Jalapeño"——OpenAI 首款专为大语言模型推理设计的定制 ASIC 芯片，从设计到流片仅历时9个月，创行业最短周期记录。该芯片采用空白设计理念，专为现代 LLM 推理工作负载量身打造，每瓦性能优于当前最优，预计于2026年底正式部署。

### Summary
On June 24, OpenAI and Broadcom jointly announced Jalapeño, OpenAI's first custom intelligence processor — a reticle-sized ASIC co-developed from initial design to manufacturing tape-out in just nine months, which the companies believe is the fastest ASIC development cycle ever achieved in high-performance semiconductors. Unlike general-purpose AI accelerators adapted from earlier workloads, Jalapeño is a blank-slate design built specifically around modern LLM inference patterns drawn from ChatGPT, Codex, the API, and agentic products. Early testing indicates performance-per-watt substantially better than current state-of-the-art; initial deployment is planned for end of 2026, with a multi-generation roadmap to follow. OpenAI used its own models to accelerate parts of the chip design process.

### Main Takeaway
