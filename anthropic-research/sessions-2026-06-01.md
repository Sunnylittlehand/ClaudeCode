### 摘要
Karpathy 在 Sequoia AI Ascent 2026 提出软件演进三阶段框架：1.0 靠显式代码，2.0 靠神经网络权重，3.0 靠提示词与代理编排。他宣告"Vibe Coding"时代已过，"Agentic Engineering"是新标准——即在保持专业质量的前提下，用代理系统大规模完成复杂任务，并以"无法转包理解力"作为结语。

### Summary
At Sequoia AI Ascent 2026, Karpathy introduced the Software 3.0 framework: Software 1.0 is explicit code written by humans; Software 2.0 is learned neural weights; Software 3.0 is agentic systems programmed through prompts, context, tools, memory, and instructions. He closed with the memorable line: "You can outsource your thinking, but you can't outsource your understanding."
