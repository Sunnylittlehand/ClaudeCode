### 摘要
OpenAI推出Presence企业级AI代理平台，整合语音与文字对话场景，内置政策护栏、操作审批、场景模拟及Codex驱动的持续优化闭环。平台在OpenAI自有英文客服热线的实测中，自主解决率达75%，上线10天内将人工转接率降低15个百分点。首批客户涵盖墨西哥BBVA、软银日本及澳大利亚零售保险公司，采用限量企业GA模式，需经OpenAI前沿部署工程师协助落地。

### Summary
OpenAI launched Presence on 22 July, a platform specifically designed for enterprises that need to deploy AI agents into production voice and chat workflows while maintaining policy control and auditability. Unlike ChatGPT Work (which focuses on internal knowledge work) or Codex (developer coding), Presence targets external-facing customer interactions and internal enterprise workflows — call centres, IT helpdesks, sales development, HR, and procurement. The platform bundles six components: company SOPs uploaded as policies; permission controls limiting which data and systems agents can access; pre-deployment simulations that test agents against common requests, edge cases, and high-risk scenarios; graders that check whether agents reached the right outcome and followed policy; escalation rules routing conversations to humans; and a Codex-powered feedback loop that reviews production interactions, proposes improvements, and requires staff approval before any change goes live. In production on OpenAI's own English phone-support line, Presence resolves 75% of inbound calls without human intervention. Within 10 days of deployment, the Codex feedback loop reduced human handoffs by 15 percentage points. Deployments require OpenAI Forward Deployed Engineers or select global systems integrators — it is explicitly not self-service. GPT-Live powers the voice channel.

### Main Takeaway
OpenAI has moved from a model API company to a managed-deployment platform company for enterprise customer-facing agents — with built-in guardrails, simulations, and a self-improving Codex feedback loop that enterprise compliance teams can actually sign off on.
