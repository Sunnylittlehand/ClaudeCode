### Summary
On 12 June 2026, the US government issued an export control directive requiring Anthropic to immediately disable all access to Fable 5 and Mythos 5 — including for Anthropic's own foreign national employees — citing a newly identified jailbreak technique as a national security concern. Anthropic complied within hours but publicly disputed the proportionality of the order, noting the jailbreak exploited only a small number of minor known vulnerabilities and did not, in their view, justify recalling models deployed to hundreds of millions of people. The directive extended to every customer globally, not just foreign nationals, because Anthropic could not segregate access quickly enough to meet the compliance timeline. This marks the first time a US government export control order has forced the complete commercial withdrawal of a deployed frontier AI model.

### Main Takeaway
The US government has demonstrated it can pull a frontier AI model from the market overnight — and it did.
