### Summary
Anthropic's Anthropic Institute published its inaugural report disclosing that Claude now writes over 80% of the code merged into Anthropic's own codebase — up from low single digits before Claude Code launched in early 2025. Co-founder Jack Clark assigned a 60% probability to fully autonomous recursive self-improvement occurring before end of 2028. The paper argues this threshold "could come sooner than most institutions are prepared for" and proposes a multi-lab, multi-country verifiable pause mechanism as a safety backstop. Critically, Anthropic frames this as a call for collective action rather than unilateral restraint: the company would slow or stop only if frontier peers verifiably did the same.

### Main Takeaway
Anthropic is simultaneously racing at the frontier and publicly warning the world it may be building something that will soon design its successors without human involvement.
