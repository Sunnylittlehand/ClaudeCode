### 摘要

### Summary
Karpathy's Sequoia AI Ascent 2026 fireside chat draws a sharp distinction between two modes of AI-assisted software creation: **vibe coding** — where anyone describes what they want and gets working software, raising the floor for non-technical builders — and **agentic engineering** — where professional developers coordinate fallible agents while preserving correctness, security, taste, and maintainability, raising the ceiling for expert practitioners. He also showcased three genuinely new capabilities LLMs unlock that were impossible with classical code: image-to-image transformation apps (menugen), natural-language installation scripts, and knowledge bases over unstructured data from arbitrary sources.
