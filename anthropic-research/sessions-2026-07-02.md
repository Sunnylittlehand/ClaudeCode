Neither metric should be read as a general capability ranking — domain-specific evaluation remains essential for regulated-industry procurement decisions.
- **The AI labour-impact story is becoming quantitative**: The Anthropic Economic Index (Cadences, 81K users) and OpenAI's "How Agents Are Transforming Work" data report mark a shift from anecdote to measurement on how AI is reshaping knowledge work; regulators and HR leaders will increasingly reference these datasets, making them directly relevant to Sunny's internal stakeholder communications.
