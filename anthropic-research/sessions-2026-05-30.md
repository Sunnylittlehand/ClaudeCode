He coined the term "vibe coding" in early 2025 and his concept frameworks (Software 1.0/2.0/3.0) are widely used across the industry. His public talks consistently set the conceptual vocabulary used by developers and product leaders for the next 6–12 months.

### Significance Rating
⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Landmark educational content: Karpathy formally declares the vibe coding era over and introduces the "agentic engineering" paradigm, providing the clearest public articulation yet of what the shift to AI-native software development actually means for professional developers and the organisations that employ them.

### 摘要
Karpathy 在红杉 AI Ascent 2026 上正式宣告"氛围编程"时代终结，提出"智能体工程"这一新范式。他将软件发展分为三个阶段：1.0（人类写代码）、2.0（人类训练神经网络）和 3.0（人类通过提示词编排 LLM，上下文窗口成为核心杠杆）。他指出，2025 年 12 月是拐点——那时智能体编程工具从"有用但混乱"跃升为"稳定输出正确代码"，他本人对 AI 的代码委托比例也从 20% 翻转至 80%。最优秀的工程师将不再是写代码最多的人，而是能在不牺牲质量的前提下最有效地指挥智能体的人。他也警告了 LLM 固有的"锯齿状能力"：能重构十万行代码库，却可能告诉你步行 50 米去洗车。

### Summary
At Sequoia's AI Ascent 2026, Karpathy formally declared vibe coding obsolete and introduced a three-part framework: **Software 1.0** (humans write explicit code), **Software 2.0** (humans create datasets and neural networks), and **Software 3.0** (humans orchestrate LLMs through prompts and context, with the context window as the primary lever). He identified December 2025 as the inflection point where agentic coding tools crossed from "helpful but messy" to consistently correct — his own delegation of code to AI inverted from 20% to 80% almost overnight. The core distinction between vibe coding and agentic engineering: vibe coding raises the floor (anyone can produce working code), while agentic engineering preserves the professional quality ceiling (great engineers direct agents without letting standards collapse). He introduced the concept of "jaggedness" — LLMs can simultaneously refactor 100K-line codebases and find zero-day vulnerabilities yet give nonsensical navigation instructions, because their capabilities are shaped by RL training circuits rather than coherent general intelligence. His final prescription: rebuild infrastructure so the primary consumer of docs, APIs, configs, and deployment flows is an agent, not a human, and the programmer's role becomes orchestrator rather than writer.

### Main Takeaway
The best engineers of the agentic era will be those who can direct AI agents to produce professional-quality output — not those who write the most code themselves.
