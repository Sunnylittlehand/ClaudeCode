## [Ben Bernanke Appointed to Anthropic's Long-Term Benefit Trust — Nobel Laureate Former Fed Chair Joins AI Governance Body](https://www.anthropic.com/news/ben-bernanke)
**Source**: Anthropic
**Type**: Corporate / Governance — Leadership Appointment
**Date**: 2026-07-09
**Links**: [Anthropic official](https://www.anthropic.com/news/ben-bernanke) · [CNBC](https://www.cnbc.com/2026/07/09/anthropic-fed-chair-bernanke-independent-trust.html) · [Bloomberg](https://www.bloomberg.com/news/articles/2026-07-09/former-fed-chairman-ben-bernanke-joins-anthropic-oversight-trust)

### Speaker / Author
Anthropic Editorial. Dr. Ben Bernanke is Distinguished Fellow at the Brookings Institution, former Chair of the US Federal Reserve (2006–2014) who stewarded monetary policy through the 2008 Global Financial Crisis, and the 2022 Nobel Prize laureate in economics (awarded for his research on banks and financial crises). He is one of the world's foremost authorities on systemic financial risk and macroeconomic policy.

### Significance Rating
⭐⭐⭐⭐ — Appointing a Nobel-laureate former central bank chair to Anthropic's independent governance trust is a deliberate signal of institutional credibility timed to the pre-IPO period, and wires macro-economic expertise directly into the body that controls a majority of Anthropic's board.

### 摘要
前美联储主席、2022年诺贝尔经济学奖得主本·伯南克已加入Anthropic长期利益信托（LTBT）。该信托是Anthropic独立治理机构，成员不持有公司股权，有权任命和罢免多数董事会成员。伯南克将重点就AI的经济影响和系统性风险向董事会提供建议，这一任命被视为Anthropic在IPO前强化治理可信度的关键举措。

### Summary
Anthropic has appointed Ben Bernanke — former Federal Reserve Chair and 2022 Nobel laureate in economics — to its Long-Term Benefit Trust (LTBT), the independent oversight body that sits above Anthropic's corporate board. Trust members hold no equity in Anthropic, receive no profit share, and are compensated only for their time; new trustees are selected by existing members rather than management or investors. Critically, the LTBT has the authority to appoint and remove a portion of Anthropic's corporate board, with that authority expanding over time until it controls a majority — a structural governance check uncommon in US technology companies. Bernanke joins Neil Buddy Shah, Richard Fontaine, and Mariano-Florentino Cuéllar on the trust. His specific mandate includes advising on the systemic economic impacts of AI and guiding Anthropic's economic research program. The appointment follows Anthropic's confidential S-1 filing in June and the launch of IPO investor meetings, suggesting Anthropic is deliberately building institutional-grade governance credibility ahead of public-market scrutiny.

### Main Takeaway
Anthropic has put a former central bank governor in charge of its ultimate governance layer — a move designed to satisfy institutional investors and regulators that the mission won't drift under public-market pressure.

⭐⭐⭐⭐ — For a regulated UK bank evaluating Anthropic as a strategic vendor, the LTBT structure is material to third-party risk assessment. The trust's independence from management and investors — and its board-control authority — is a governance safeguard that Sunny can point to in her FCA/PRA supplier risk framework: it provides a structural check on value-extraction behaviour post-IPO that most comparable technology vendors lack. Sunny should reference the LTBT structure in her vendor due-diligence documentation.
