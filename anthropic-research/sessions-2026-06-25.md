### 摘要
Anthropic于2026年6月23日正式推出Claude Tag——一款深度整合Slack的企业级异步AI智能体。与传统每位用户私用Claude不同，Claude Tag以"团队成员"身份加入Slack工作区，拥有独立身份、持久记忆与工具访问权限。团队成员只需@Claude即可分配任务，Claude可自主拆解为里程碑、调用工具、异步完成并在线程中汇报结果。Anthropic内部数据显示，其产品团队65%的代码已由内部版Claude Tag生成。该功能目前以研究预览形式向Enterprise和Team客户开放，旧版Claude for Slack集成将于8月3日停用。

### Summary
Anthropic launched Claude Tag on June 23 as a research preview for Enterprise and Team customers: a Slack-native agent that joins a workspace as a persistent team member rather than a per-user private assistant. Teammates @mention Claude to assign tasks, which it breaks into milestones, executes asynchronously with connected tools, and reports back in-thread — the team can move on and Claude catches up. An ambient monitoring mode lets Claude proactively surface relevant information without being tagged. Anthropic reports that 65% of its own product team's code is now produced by an internal version of Claude Tag, and the legacy Claude in Slack integration will be retired August 3, 2026.

### Main Takeaway
Claude just became a full-time Slack colleague — not a chatbot in your sidebar, but an async team member with its own memory, tools, and a seat in every channel.
