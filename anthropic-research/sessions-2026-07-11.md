### Speaker / Author
OpenAI Editorial. GPT-Live is a new product family distinct from the existing gpt-realtime series; it represents OpenAI's push into natural real-time voice interaction, announced two days before the full GPT-5.6 GA rollout.

### Significance Rating
⭐⭐⭐⭐ — The first truly full-duplex AI voice model in ChatGPT: it listens and speaks simultaneously rather than turn-taking, removing the artificial pauses that made earlier voice AI feel robotic.

### 摘要
OpenAI于7月8日发布GPT-Live系列语音模型，实现真正全双工通话：模型可同时聆听和说话，以"嗯""好的"等回应表示关注，并在用户需要时保持沉默。GPT-Live-1面向付费用户，GPT-Live-1 mini面向免费用户，复杂任务后台调用GPT-5.5处理。API接入计划近期推出。这标志着AI语音交互体验的重大升级，接近真实人类对话质感。

### Summary
OpenAI launched GPT-Live on July 8, rolling out two versions globally across ChatGPT: GPT-Live-1 (default for paid users) and GPT-Live-1 mini (for Free tier). Unlike previous voice modes that wait for the user to finish speaking before responding, GPT-Live is architecturally full-duplex — it processes input continuously while generating output, enabling sub-second interaction decisions (speak, listen, pause, interrupt, or invoke a tool). For questions requiring deeper reasoning or web search, GPT-Live delegates to GPT-5.5 in the background. API access was not available at launch; developers can sign up for early access notification. GPT-Live is a distinct product from gpt-realtime-2.1 (a latency-optimised real-time API released July 6); the two address different use cases.

### Main Takeaway
OpenAI has leapfrogged turn-based voice AI with a full-duplex model that feels like a real conversation — raising the capability bar for voice interfaces across the industry.

### Relevance to Sunny
⭐⭐⭐ — Worth tracking. Voice AI in investment banking is currently constrained by MiFID II call-recording requirements and comms surveillance obligations; any bank deploying voice AI for client-facing or trading-floor use would need the agent's voice output treated as a captured communication. That regulatory barrier means GPT-Live's near-term enterprise adoption in UK financial services is limited. However, for internal use cases (meeting assistants, research summaries, internal helpdesks) the technology is directly applicable. Anthropic does not currently have a comparable full-duplex voice offering, so if OpenAI brings GPT-Live to the ChatGPT Enterprise API, it becomes a meaningful feature gap that Sunny may face when developer teams ask why Claude doesn't have equivalent voice capability.
